Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer for landlords?

We offer a comprehensive range of services including Tenant Find, Rent Collect, and Fully Managed options. Each package is designed to suit different landlord needs, from finding quality tenants to complete property management.

How do you screen tenants?

We conduct thorough tenant referencing including credit checks, employment verification, previous landlord references, and right to rent checks to ensure you get reliable tenants.

What happens if my tenant doesn't pay rent?

Our team handles all rent arrears professionally. We have established procedures for chasing late payments and, if necessary, can guide you through the legal process to recover owed rent.

How often do you inspect properties?

For fully managed properties, we conduct routine inspections every 3-6 months. This helps identify any maintenance issues early and ensures tenants are taking good care of your property.
