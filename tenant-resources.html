Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to rent a property?

You'll typically need proof of identity (passport or driving licence), proof of address, proof of income (payslips or bank statements), and references from previous landlords or employers.

We can guide you through the specific requirements for your situation.

How much deposit do I need to pay?

The deposit is typically equivalent to 5 weeks' rent for annual rentals under £50,000. Your deposit will be protected in a government-approved scheme.

What is included in my rent?

This varies by property. Some rentals include bills like water, gas, electricity, and council tax, while others are rent-only. Each property listing will specify what's included.

How do I report maintenance issues?

You can report maintenance issues by contacting our office directly via phone or email. For emergencies outside office hours, we have an out-of-hours contact number provided in your tenancy welcome pack.
