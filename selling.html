Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to sell a property?

The time it takes to sell a property varies depending on market conditions, pricing, and property appeal. On average, properties in our area sell within 8-12 weeks from listing to completion.

We work to ensure your property gets maximum exposure to speed up the process.

What fees are involved in selling my property?

Our estate agent fees are competitive and transparent. You'll also need to budget for solicitor fees, EPC certificate, and any necessary repairs or staging costs.

Do I need an EPC to sell my property?

Yes, an Energy Performance Certificate (EPC) is legally required when selling a property. We can help arrange this for you as part of our service.

How do you market my property?

We use professional photography, virtual tours, and list on major property portals including Rightmove, Zoopla, and OnTheMarket. We also promote through social media and our database of active buyers.
